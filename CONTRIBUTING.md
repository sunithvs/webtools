## How to Contribute

1. **Fork the repository**.
2. **Create a new branch** for your feature or bug fix: `git checkout -b feature/amazing-feature`.
3. **Make your changes**.
4. **Test your changes** by reloading the extension in `chrome://extensions`.
5. **Commit your changes**: `git commit -m 'Add some amazing feature'`.
6. **Push to the branch**: `git push origin feature/amazing-feature`.
7. **Submit a Pull Request**.

## ideas?

If you have ideas for new efficiency metrics or UI improvements, feel free to open an issue or start a discussion!
